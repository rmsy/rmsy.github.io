Privacy Policy for Touch Grass Discord App

Welcome to Touch Grass. Touch Grass is a Discord integration owned and operated by Isaac Moore (Moore Media). The App allows users to link their Discord profile with their Strava account and automatically sync Strava activities to designated Discord servers.

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, how long we retain it, and the rights available to you. Last updated: November 1, 2025.

I. INFORMATION CONTROLLER / OPERATOR

Touch Grass is operated by Isaac Moore (Moore Media).
Contact: im@isaacmoore.me

II. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service and enable the Strava ↔ Discord integration. This includes, but is not strictly limited to:

  • Account identifiers: Discord user ID and Strava user ID.
  • OAuth tokens: Tokens required by Discord and Strava's API's to access authorized data.
  • Strava activity data: Activity metadata such as title, type (ride/run), distance, duration, start time, and public description.
  • Server configuration: Which Discord server/channel you have chosen and any personal preferences.
  • Logs and diagnostics: Short-term logs for debugging, delivery status of posted messages, error reports, and basic analytics.

Important: We do not collect or store your Discord or Strava password. All access is performed through their respective OAuth flows.

III. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and link your Discord and Strava accounts (OAuth).
  • Retrieve authorized Strava activities and post summary messages to the Discord server or channel you specify.
  • Maintain and refresh tokens so syncing continues as authorized.
  • Monitor, debug, and improve the App (error logs, analytics about sync operations).
  • Comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, or protect against fraud.

IV. LEGAL BASES FOR PROCESSING (EU / EEA USERS)

If you are an EU/EEA resident, our lawful bases for processing personal data include:

  • Consent – when you authorize Touch Grass via OAuth.
  • Performance of a contract – to provide the integration you request.
  • Legitimate interests – for analytics, troubleshooting, and improving the service, where such interests are not overridden by your rights.

V. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Third-party services: Strava and Discord. We use their APIs to read and post data; their privacy policies apply to those services.
  • Service providers: Hosting, analytics, and monitoring providers who process data on our behalf under contract.
  • Legal obligations: To comply with law, subpoenas, court orders, or to prevent fraud or imminent harm.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to confidentiality protections.

VII. SECURITY

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data, including access controls and secure hosting. However, no internet service is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

VIII. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access to, correction of, deletion of, restriction of processing of, or portability of your personal data. You may also have the right to object to processing and to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise any rights or request deletion, contact us at im@isaacmoore.me. You may also revoke Touch Grass's access via your Discord or Strava account settings.

IX. CHILDREN

Touch Grass does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we obtain knowledge of such data, we will delete it promptly.

X. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
